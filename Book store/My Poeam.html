The March of the Earth

Earth, you’re nestled in twilight and
from your slumber flows of lava
erupted awake in fury to the drumbeat of a stately march
oozing over the slothful time of budding shoots
and bones saturated in humus-rich soil
follow the rhythm of your vapors and dance with me,
Earth let us lose ourselves
let us lose ourselves
in a delirium
I saved some of your jasmine fragrance, Earth
smear it onto my breasts
and make love to me
till you’ve pulled my hair straight into your heart
I feed your spawn
I, time of eternity
give birth to the new world
